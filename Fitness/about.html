Common Exercises

The Following are common exercises that we encourage our clients to do as part of their daily exercise routine.

Burpee
Burpees are a great, full body exercise to increase your strenght and endurance. Begin in a standing position, drop into a squad and extend your hand forward, kick your feet back and forward again quickly and then jump from a squatted position.
Plank
Planks build your core strenght. To perform a plank, get in a push up position and rest your forearms on the floor. Hold the position as long as you can
Mountain Climber
Mountain climbers are a good cardio exercise. Place your hands on the the floor for a push up position., than switchas quickly
